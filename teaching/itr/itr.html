Introduction to Robotics

Welcome to the course page for Introduction to Robotics. This page contains all course realted information for students taking the course. This is a self study course with assignments, quizes and exams provided by the instructor. For all course related queries please contact the course instructor.

Course Information Document

You can download the course document information from here.

Lecture timings:

There will be no formal lectues offered for this course. You are required to follow the online lectures on Introduction to Robotics by Prof. Oussama Khatib, Stanford University.

Reference textbooks:

There is no one fixed reference textbook for the course. However, you are strongly encouraged to refer to the following textbooks for the different topics covered in the course.

  • Spong, M. W., & Vidyasagar, M. (2008). Robot dynamics and control. John Wiley & Sons.
  • Murray, R. M., Li, Z., Sastry, S. S., & Sastry, S. S. (1994). A mathematical introduction to robotic manipulation. CRC press. Online book
  • Siciliano, B., Sciavicco, L., Villani, L., & Oriolo, G. (2010). Robotics: modelling, planning and control. Springer Science & Business Media.

Other online materials:

You are also enouraged to make use of the following online video lectures, if you can.

  • Online course on Linear Algebra by G Straing at MIT OCW. Course Link
  • Online course on Linear Dynamical Systems by S Boyd. Course Link
